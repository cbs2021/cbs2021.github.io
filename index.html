Welcome to Zoom University!

As one of the oldest business school in the nation, Zoom University was founded back in 1880 as Columbia Buinsess School. It was officially renamed to Zoom University during the Coronavirus pandemic of 2020. Today, it belongs to the an extensive network of top 7 business schools, known as the Zoom Consortium.
